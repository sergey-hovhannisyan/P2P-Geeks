About Us

P2P Geeks was designed and developed by a group of undergraduate computer science students looking for a better way to prepare for interviews. The team has analyzed various studies and experiences to truly understand the reasons this process has been so daunting and difficult. Performance anxiety was identified as one of the primary causes for failing a technical interview, and so the team decided that the best way to attack that issue was to offer a solution that enables applicatns to perform in real-time for free.
